Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my account not verified?

Your account may not be verified for the following reasons:

  • You have not completed the email verification process. Check your inbox for the verification link.
  • If you're an employee, your account will need to be approved by an admin. Please wait for further confirmation.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

What should I do if I encounter a technical issue?

If you experience any technical difficulties, please contact our support team at support@example.com.
